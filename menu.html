Welcome to Happy Life Happy Dining!

Come And Feast Your Eyes Upon These Delicious Meals!

Rose Tteokboki Pasta

Our Rose Tteokbokki Pasta is made of chewy Korean rice cakes that have been simmered in spicy and sweet sauce. This dish leaves a nice creamy and tingly aftertaste which is perfect for those with low spice tolerance.

Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani

Our Hyderabadi Chicken Biryani is made with basmati rice and chicken that has been marinated in spices. This Indian Dish is a perfect meal to make for gatherings offering a flavorful and spicy taste.

Vegetarian Japanese Ramen

This vegetarian friendly ramen is made using soy milk, miso, sesame seeds, and spicy chilli bean sauce. This dish has a nice creamy and rich broth that is achieved by using plant-based ingredients and is meat free. Be sure to enjoy this on a nice cold day!

Baked Niangao

Baked Niangao is a Chinese traditional-steamed rice cake. This is usually served during the Lunar New Year that offers a sweet, soft, and springy texture when eaten. This is simpler version that can easily be made in record time!

Gulab Jamun

This sweet and refreshing Indian dessert has come your way to give your taste buds a burst of sweetness. This sweet is made with khoya (milk solids), sugar, rose water, and cardamom powder. This is Chef's special dish and is regarded as a popular dish.
